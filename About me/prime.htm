Prime/Composite Number

This program finds whether a given number is prime or not.

Prime No.: Prime numbers are numbers that have only 2 factors: 1 and themselves. For example, the first 5 prime numbers are 2, 3, 5, 7, and 11.

Composite No.: Numbers with more than 2 factors are call composite numbers. For Example, the first 5 composite numbers are 4, 6, 8, 9, 10.
