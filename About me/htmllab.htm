Now, as you have learned about my hobbies, let's discuss how I acquired them and why I love them.

1.) Cricket -  Like majority of the Indians I am also fond of cricket. I don't exactly remember how I acquired this hobby but I think it is because of two reasons, firstly my father is also a cricket lover and secondly I saw India become the World Champions of cricket in 2011. The second one I think helped in embedding cricket in mmy heart. Even today whenever I get time I go out and play cricket with my friends(Gully cricket). Talking about my favourite cricketer, he is the one and only Mahendra Singh Dhoni.


2.) Basketball -  I used to play basketball in the games period in my school with my friends and learnt a lot about this game from them. Some of my friends were even in the school basketball team and they used to teach me the basics of the game. This is how I developed basketball as a hobby.

3.) Kabaddi - Though I knew how to play kabaddi before but it was only after the first season of the Pro kabaddi league that I started developing interst in the game. The main reason why I started watching kabaddi was that that my city got it's own franchise namely the Jaipur Pink Panthers which went on to win the inaugural season of PKL. I love the intensity and the furiosity with which the game is played and this is the reason how I acquired it as my hobby.
